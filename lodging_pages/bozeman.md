The town serves as a popular basecamp for exploring Yellowstone National Park, skiing at Bridger Bowl and Big Sky Resort, and various outdoor activities like fly-fishing and hiking in the surrounding mountains.

## What to Know Before You Book

Peak booking periods in Bozeman are during the summer (July-August) and ski season (winter), especially around holidays and major events. It is highly recommended to book accommodations well in advance during these times. Many downtown hotels offer valet parking, but for other areas, it's advisable to check parking availability with the accommodation directly. Seasonal pricing patterns mean higher rates during peak seasons and potentially lower rates during shoulder seasons (May-June and September-October), which can also offer fewer crowds.
