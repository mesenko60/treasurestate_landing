# Belgrade vs Bigfork: Which Montana Town is Right for You?

Belgrade and Bigfork are both desirable Montana communities, but they offer very different mountain lifestyles on opposite sides of the Continental Divide. Belgrade is the Hub of the Gallatin Valley, a fast-growing suburb of 12,509 in Gallatin County at 4,462 feet, with Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport 5 miles away, 104 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, 15 lakes, 21 fishing access sites, 2 hot springs, a national forest, a ski area, and a 3,800-student school district. Bigfork is a Village by the Bay, a small resort community of 5,000 in Flathead County at 2,940 feet on the northeastern shore of Flathead Lake, with Glacier Park International Airport 22 miles away, 176 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 32 lakes, 28 fishing access sites, 27 campgrounds, 25 trailheads, 16 state parks, 10 museums, 9 golf courses, 8 nature reserves, 6 historic sites, 4 disc golf courses, 4 boat launches, 1 national park, 1 ski area, and a 900-student school district.

## Who Should Move to Belgrade?

*   Families and professionals who want Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport 5 miles away, a 3,800-student school district, and all the amenities of the Bozeman metro area at a slightly lower price than Bozeman itself
*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, 15 lakes, 21 fishing access sites, 2 hot springs, a ski area, and a national forest all within 30 miles, with quick access to the Bridger Mountains and Gallatin Canyon
*   Buyers who want a rapidly growing community with strong job access in the Gallatin Valley, with a typical home value of $544,626 and a median household income of $88,896
*   Remote workers and young professionals who want a suburban Gallatin Valley lifestyle with proximity to Bozeman's restaurants, university, and cultural amenities without paying Bozeman prices

## Who Should Move to Bigfork?

*   Outdoor enthusiasts and lake lovers who want 32 lakes, 28 fishing access sites, 27 campgrounds, 25 trailheads, 16 state parks, 4 boat launches, and Glacier National Park all within 30 miles, on the shore of Flathead Lake, one of the largest natural freshwater lakes in the American West
*   Arts and culture enthusiasts drawn to Bigfork's well-established arts community, summer theater, galleries, and a vibrant village downtown that punches well above its size
*   Retirees, remote workers, and affluent buyers who want a small, scenic resort community on Flathead Lake with outstanding outdoor access, with a typical home value of $758,435 and a median household income of $82,648
*   Anyone who wants a genuine village atmosphere with a 900-student school district, 10 museums, 9 golf courses, and Glacier National Park nearby, in one of the most beautiful settings in Montana

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Bigfork is more expensive than Belgrade for home purchases. The typical home value in Bigfork is $758,435, compared to $544,626 in Belgrade, about $214,000 more. Rent runs in the opposite direction: Belgrade averages $2,206 per month versus $1,103 in Bigfork, reflecting Belgrade's tight rental market driven by Gallatin Valley demand.

Median household income is higher in Belgrade at $88,896 versus $82,648 in Bigfork. Both communities are relatively affluent by Montana standards, but Bigfork's higher home prices reflect its status as a premier Flathead Lake resort community.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Belgrade feels like a prosperous, growing suburb of Bozeman, with new subdivisions, strong outdoor access, and a community identity tied closely to the Gallatin Valley's outdoor and university culture. Bigfork is a small, artsy lakeside village where Flathead Lake, the Swan River, a summer theater tradition, and a strong arts scene define community life. Bigfork has 32 lakes and 16 state parks within 30 miles that Belgrade cannot match. Belgrade has 14 waterfalls and 2 hot springs that Bigfork does not. If you want a fast-growing Gallatin Valley community with outstanding mountain access and a suburban feel, Belgrade is one of the best values in the Bozeman area. If you want a scenic, artsy village on Flathead Lake with extraordinary outdoor diversity and a community that feels genuinely special, Bigfork is one of the most charming places to live in Montana.
