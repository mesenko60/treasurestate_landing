# Glendive vs Whitefish: Which Montana Community is Right for You?

Glendive and Whitefish represent two very different ends of the Montana lifestyle spectrum. Glendive is the Paddlefish Capital of the World, a community of 4,873 in Dawson County in eastern Montana at 2,083 feet elevation, with 30 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles and Makoshika State Park at its edge. Whitefish is Stumptown, a resort community of 8,915 in Flathead County in western Montana at 3,038 feet, with 202 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 46 campgrounds, 33 trailheads, 26 lakes, 22 fishing access sites, 19 ski areas, 8 state parks, 7 waterfalls, Glacier National Park nearby, and Whitefish Mountain Resort as its anchor.

## Who Should Move to Glendive?

*   Budget-conscious buyers who want Montana at a very low price, with a typical home value of $185,447 and median rent of just $832 per month, compared to Whitefish's $840,135 and $2,639
*   Outdoor enthusiasts and anglers who want Makoshika State Park, the Yellowstone River, and some of the best paddlefish snagging in the country right in their backyard
*   People who want a small, quiet eastern Montana community with a genuine small-town character and a slower pace of life
*   Remote workers or retirees who want affordable housing and a community where $71,063 in median household income goes a very long way

## Who Should Move to Whitefish?

*   Skiers and outdoor enthusiasts who want Whitefish Mountain Resort, 19 ski areas within 30 miles, 46 campgrounds, 33 trailheads, 7 waterfalls, and Glacier National Park all within reach
*   People who want a lively small-town downtown with restaurants, breweries, boutiques, and a year-round resort energy that few Montana towns can match
*   Families who want a 1,800-student school district and a community that has built a strong identity around both winter and summer outdoor recreation
*   Anyone drawn to Whitefish Lake, the resort lifestyle, and Kalispell's full range of services just 9 miles away

## Cost of Living Breakdown

The gap between these two towns is enormous. The typical home value in Glendive is around $185,447, compared to $840,135 in Whitefish, more than four times the price. Rent is similarly dramatic: Glendive averages about $832 per month, while Whitefish comes in at roughly $2,639, more than three times as much.

Median household incomes are nearly identical: $71,063 in Glendive versus $71,110 in Whitefish. That means residents of both towns earn about the same, but Whitefish costs dramatically more to live in.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Glendive is a small eastern Montana town where the Yellowstone River, Makoshika State Park, and the paddlefish season define the calendar. Whitefish is a resort town where Whitefish Mountain Resort, Glacier National Park, Whitefish Lake, and a lively downtown define daily life. These two towns are about as different as Montana gets in terms of cost and character. If you want affordability and small-town quiet in eastern Montana, Glendive is a genuine option. If you want the resort town energy, the ski mountain, and one of the most beautiful settings in the American Northwest, Whitefish is exceptional.
