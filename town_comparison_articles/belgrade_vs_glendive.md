# Belgrade vs Glendive: Which Montana Town is Right for You?

Belgrade and Glendive are both mid-sized Montana communities, but they sit at opposite ends of the state and represent very different Montana lifestyles. Belgrade is the Hub of the Gallatin Valley, a fast-growing suburb of 12,509 in Gallatin County at 4,462 feet, with Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport 5 miles away, 104 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, 15 lakes, 21 fishing access sites, 2 hot springs, a national forest, a ski area, and a 3,800-student school district. Glendive is the Paddlefish Capital of the World, a small eastern Montana city of 4,873 in Dawson County at 2,083 feet on the Yellowstone River, with Glendive Airport 6 miles away, 30 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 7 viewpoints, 6 museums, 4 fishing access sites, 3 campgrounds, 3 lakes, 2 state parks, 1 disc golf course, 1 golf course, 1 historic site, 1 nature reserve, and a 1,000-student school district.

## Who Should Move to Belgrade?

*   Families and professionals who want Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport 5 miles away, a 3,800-student school district, and all the amenities of the Bozeman metro area at a slightly lower price than Bozeman itself
*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, 15 lakes, 21 fishing access sites, 2 hot springs, a ski area, and a national forest all within 30 miles, with quick access to the Bridger Mountains and Gallatin Canyon
*   Buyers who want a rapidly growing community with strong job access in the Gallatin Valley, with a typical home value of $544,626 and a median household income of $88,896
*   Remote workers and young professionals who want a suburban Gallatin Valley lifestyle with proximity to Bozeman's restaurants, university, and cultural amenities without paying Bozeman prices

## Who Should Move to Glendive?

*   Anglers and paddlefish enthusiasts who want access to the Yellowstone River and one of the most unique fishing experiences in North America, in a community that celebrates its paddlefish heritage every spring
*   Fossil hunters and geology enthusiasts drawn to Makoshika State Park, Montana's largest state park, with its dramatic badlands formations, dinosaur fossils, and 7 viewpoints within 30 miles
*   Budget-conscious buyers who want one of the most affordable housing markets in Montana, with a typical home value of $185,447 and rent averaging just $832 per month
*   Oil and gas industry workers, agricultural professionals, and anyone who wants a quiet, affordable eastern Montana community with a strong local economy and a 1,000-student school district

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Belgrade is dramatically more expensive than Glendive. The typical home value in Belgrade is $544,626, compared to $185,447 in Glendive, nearly three times higher. Rent reflects the same gap: Belgrade averages $2,206 per month versus $832 in Glendive, more than two and a half times higher.

Median household income is higher in Belgrade at $88,896 versus $71,063 in Glendive. Glendive offers some of the most affordable housing in Montana, making it accessible to buyers on almost any budget. Belgrade's higher costs reflect its position in the booming Gallatin Valley market.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Belgrade feels like a prosperous, growing suburb of Bozeman, with new subdivisions, strong outdoor access, and a community identity tied closely to the Gallatin Valley's outdoor and university culture. Glendive is a quiet eastern Montana river town where the Yellowstone River, Makoshika State Park's badlands, paddlefish season, and a strong agricultural and energy economy define community life. Belgrade has 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, and a ski area within 30 miles that Glendive cannot match. Glendive has 7 viewpoints and 6 museums that reflect its unique badlands and fossil heritage, including the Makoshika State Park Visitors Center. If you want a fast-growing Gallatin Valley community with outstanding mountain access and a suburban feel, Belgrade is one of the best values in the Bozeman area. If you want an affordable eastern Montana river town with unique geological character, legendary paddlefish fishing, and a community that is genuinely off the beaten path, Glendive is one of the most distinctive places to live in Montana.
