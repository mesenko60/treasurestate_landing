# Libby vs Billings, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Libby and Billings offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Libby is a remote timber town in the Kootenai National Forest with 2,775 people, its deep roots in the logging industry, its stunning, rugged isolation, and quick access to the Kootenai River and Cabinet Mountains. Billings, with just 117,445 residents, is a Montana's largest city known for its major medical centers, its sprawling retail sector, and the Rimrocks and Yellowstone River.

## Who Should Move to Libby?

* Outdoor enthusiasts seeking uncrowded access to some of Montana's most rugged and remote wilderness.
* Retirees and remote workers looking for affordable mountain living far from major tourist hubs.
* People who appreciate a tight-knit, resilient community with a strong working-class heritage.
* Hunters, anglers, and hikers who want to live in the heart of the Kootenai National Forest.

## Who Should Move to Billings?

* Professionals seeking corporate or medical careers.
* Families wanting large school districts and diverse activities.
* People who prefer urban conveniences over small-town quiet.
* Those who want easy access to a major regional airport.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Libby is around $285,000, while in Billings it is closer to $375,000, a difference of roughly $90,000, making Libby significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Libby averages about $850 per month, compared to roughly $1,200 in Billings.

Libby's overall cost of living differs from Billings's. Libby offers lower average salaries compared to Billings, with a median household income of $45,000 compared to Billings's $65,000.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Libby is a resilient, isolated town tucked into the far northwest corner of the state. It is defined by its timber heritage and its incredible, uncrowded access to the Kootenai River and Cabinet Mountains, offering a quiet, rugged lifestyle. Billings is a bustling regional hub with a true city feel, offering extensive amenities and a fast-paced environment.
