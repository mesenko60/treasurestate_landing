# Miles City vs Glendive, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Miles City and Glendive offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Miles City is a ranching community on the eastern plains with 8,412 people, the livestock industry, rodeo culture, and quick access to the wide-open Yellowstone River country. Glendive, with just 4,873 residents, is a rugged eastern Montana town known for its rich dinosaur fossil beds, its position as an agricultural and energy hub, and Makoshika State Park and the Yellowstone River.

## Who Should Move to Miles City?

* Ranchers, livestock producers, and people whose careers and lifestyle are rooted in Eastern Montana's agricultural economy.
* Those seeking very affordable housing in a community where the typical home costs less than half what it does in many western Montana towns.
* Remote workers or retirees who want quiet, uncrowded living without the cost pressures that come with proximity to major tourist hubs.
* People who appreciate the authentic cowboy heritage of the Yellowstone Valley and the Bucking Horse Sale rodeo tradition.

## Who Should Move to Glendive?

* Workers in the agriculture, railroad, or energy sectors looking for steady employment in eastern Montana.
* Homebuyers seeking some of the most affordable real estate in the state.
* People who appreciate the stark beauty of the badlands and the quiet isolation of the high plains.
* Paleontology enthusiasts and rockhounds drawn to the fossil-rich terrain of Makoshika State Park.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Miles City is around $233,769, while in Glendive it is closer to $185,447, a difference of roughly $48,322, making Glendive significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Miles City averages about $750 per month, compared to roughly $832 in Glendive.

Miles City's overall cost of living differs from Glendive's. Miles City offers lower average salaries compared to Glendive, with a median household income of $60,732 compared to Glendive's $71,063.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Miles City is quiet and agricultural in character, with a lifestyle shaped by ranching seasons and rodeo. It sits along the Yellowstone River, looking east toward the open plains. Glendive is a practical, hardworking town set against the dramatic backdrop of the Montana badlands. It is far removed from the tourist crowds of the western mountains, offering a quiet, resilient community life where the local economy is tied to the land, the railroad, and the river.
