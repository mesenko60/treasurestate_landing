# Billings vs Anaconda: Which Montana Community is Right for You?

Billings and Anaconda are about as different as two Montana communities can get. Billings is the Magic City, Montana's largest city at 117,116 people, a modern commercial hub with a diversified economy, a busy airport, and all the amenities of a regional capital. Anaconda is the Smelter City, a small, historically rich copper-smelting town of 9,421 tucked into the mountains of southwestern Montana at 5,279 feet, with some of the most affordable housing in the state and a remarkable outdoor recreation landscape just outside the door.

## Who Should Move to Billings?

*   Professionals who need access to Montana's largest and most diverse job market
*   Families who want a full-service city with major hospitals, strong schools, and big-city amenities
*   People who prefer a warmer, drier climate and easy access to Yellowstone Country
*   Anyone who values a regional airport, major retailers, and the full infrastructure of a large city

## Who Should Move to Anaconda?

*   Remote workers and retirees seeking some of Montana's most affordable housing in a scenic mountain setting
*   History enthusiasts drawn to Anaconda's extraordinary copper-boom architecture and the Anaconda Smelter Stack State Park
*   Outdoor lovers who want hot springs, mountain lakes, wilderness, and ski access within 30 miles
*   People who want a quiet, tight-knit community with a strong working-class identity and genuine Montana character

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Anaconda is one of the most affordable communities in Montana. The median home price is around $283,380, nearly $110,000 less than Billings' $390,654. But the most striking difference is rent: a typical apartment in Anaconda runs about $661 per month, compared to $1,383 in Billings, less than half the cost.

The trade-off is income. Billings' median household income of $71,855 is nearly $22,000 higher than Anaconda's $49,533, reflecting the significant difference in local job markets. For anyone whose income isn't tied to local employment, Anaconda's affordability is a genuine and compelling advantage.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Billings moves fast, always building, always busy, with the energy of a city that knows it's the economic engine of the region. Anaconda moves at a completely different pace. It's a town that has made peace with its industrial past and found a new identity in its natural surroundings and its history. The Old Works Golf Course, built on the site of the old smelter works, is a symbol of that reinvention, a world-class Jack Nicklaus-designed course that uses the industrial ruins as course features. It's the kind of place that surprises people who expect a former mining town and find something far more interesting.
