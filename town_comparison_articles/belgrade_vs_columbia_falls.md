# Belgrade vs Columbia Falls: Which Montana Town is Right for You?

Belgrade and Columbia Falls are both growing Montana communities, but they sit in different mountain corridors and attract different residents. Belgrade is the Hub of the Gallatin Valley, a fast-growing suburb of 12,509 in Gallatin County at 4,462 feet, with Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport 5 miles away, 104 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, 15 lakes, 21 fishing access sites, 2 hot springs, a national forest, a ski area, and a 3,800-student school district. Columbia Falls is the Gateway to Glacier, a western Montana community of 5,308 in Flathead County at 3,077 feet, with Glacier Park International Airport 6 miles away, 214 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 48 campgrounds, 31 trailheads, 27 fishing access sites, 26 lakes, 19 ski areas, 11 waterfalls, 9 state parks, 8 museums, 8 golf courses, 6 nature reserves, 4 historic sites, 4 disc golf courses, 4 boat launches, 1 national park, and a 1,800-student school district.

## Who Should Move to Belgrade?

*   Families and professionals who want Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport 5 miles away, a 3,800-student school district, and all the amenities of the Bozeman metro area at a slightly lower price than Bozeman itself
*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 16 trailheads, 14 waterfalls, 15 lakes, 21 fishing access sites, 2 hot springs, a ski area, and a national forest all within 30 miles, with quick access to the Bridger Mountains and Gallatin Canyon
*   Buyers who want a rapidly growing community with strong job access in the Gallatin Valley, with a typical home value of $544,626 and a median household income of $88,896
*   Remote workers and young professionals who want a suburban Gallatin Valley lifestyle with proximity to Bozeman's restaurants, university, and cultural amenities without paying Bozeman prices

## Who Should Move to Columbia Falls?

*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 48 campgrounds, 31 trailheads, 27 fishing access sites, 26 lakes, 19 ski areas, 11 waterfalls, 9 state parks, and Glacier National Park all within 30 miles, in one of the most scenically spectacular corners of Montana
*   Families and professionals who want Glacier Park International Airport 6 miles away, a 1,800-student school district, 8 museums, 8 golf courses, and a median household income of $65,313 in a genuine western Montana community
*   Buyers who want to live near Glacier National Park and the Flathead Valley at a more affordable price than Whitefish or Bigfork, with a typical home value of $584,580
*   Anyone who wants a small, community-oriented western Montana town with extraordinary outdoor diversity and the Flathead River, Whitefish Mountain Resort, and Glacier National Park all within easy reach

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Belgrade and Columbia Falls are surprisingly close in home prices. The typical home value in Belgrade is $544,626, compared to $584,580 in Columbia Falls, with Columbia Falls actually running slightly higher. Rent is dramatically different: Belgrade averages $2,206 per month versus $1,003 in Columbia Falls, more than double.

Median household income is higher in Belgrade at $88,896 versus $65,313 in Columbia Falls. Columbia Falls offers a rare combination of outstanding outdoor access and relatively affordable rents, making it one of the better values in the Flathead Valley.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Belgrade feels like a prosperous, growing suburb of Bozeman, with new subdivisions, strong outdoor access, and a community identity tied closely to the Gallatin Valley's outdoor and university culture. Columbia Falls is a smaller, more working-class western Montana community where Glacier National Park, the Flathead River, and a strong outdoor recreation economy define daily life. Columbia Falls has 214 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles compared to Belgrade's 104, including 19 ski areas and 1 national park that Belgrade cannot match. Belgrade has 14 waterfalls and 2 hot springs that Columbia Falls does not. If you want a fast-growing Gallatin Valley community with outstanding mountain access and a suburban feel, Belgrade is one of the best values in the Bozeman area. If you want a genuine western Montana community with extraordinary outdoor diversity and Glacier National Park in your backyard, Columbia Falls is one of the most compelling small towns in the Flathead Valley.
