# Glendive vs Anaconda: Which Montana Community is Right for You?

Glendive and Anaconda are both small Montana communities with affordable housing and strong local identities, but they sit in very different landscapes. Glendive is the Paddlefish Capital of the World, a community of 4,873 in Dawson County in eastern Montana at 2,083 feet elevation, with 30 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles and Makoshika State Park at its edge. Anaconda is the Smelter City, a community of 9,421 in Deer Lodge County in western Montana at 5,279 feet, with 134 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 48 lakes, 19 campgrounds, 16 museums, 9 trailheads, 8 fishing access sites, a ski area, a hot spring at Fairmont Hot Springs, and one of the most distinctive copper smelting heritage landscapes in the American West.

## Who Should Move to Glendive?

*   Budget-conscious buyers who want Montana at a very low price, with a typical home value of $185,447 and median rent of just $832 per month
*   Outdoor enthusiasts and anglers who want Makoshika State Park, the Yellowstone River, and some of the best paddlefish snagging in the country right in their backyard
*   People who want a small, quiet eastern Montana community with a genuine small-town character and a slower pace of life
*   Remote workers or retirees who want affordable housing and a community where $71,063 in median household income goes a very long way

## Who Should Move to Anaconda?

*   Budget-conscious buyers who want western Montana at a very low price, with a typical home value of $283,380 and median rent of just $661 per month, the lowest of any community in this comparison set
*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 48 lakes, 19 campgrounds, 9 trailheads, 8 fishing access sites, a ski area at Discovery Mountain, a hot spring at Fairmont Hot Springs, and Lost Creek Falls all within 30 miles
*   History enthusiasts drawn to Anaconda's extraordinary copper smelting heritage, the Anaconda Smelter Stack State Park, the Old Works Golf Course built on the historic smelter site, and 16 museums within 30 miles
*   People who want a small Montana community with a strong sense of place, easy access to Butte and Deer Lodge, and some of the most affordable housing in western Montana

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Both towns are affordable by Montana standards, but they differ in important ways. The typical home value in Glendive is around $185,447, compared to $283,380 in Anaconda, about 53% higher. Rent is actually lower in Anaconda: Glendive averages about $832 per month, while Anaconda comes in at roughly $661.

Median household income is considerably higher in Glendive at $71,063 versus $49,533 in Anaconda. Glendive residents earn significantly more on average, which makes both towns competitive on affordability despite the home price difference.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Glendive is a small eastern Montana town where the Yellowstone River, Makoshika State Park, and the paddlefish season define the calendar. Anaconda is a community shaped by copper, labor history, and the Pintler Mountains, with a gritty authenticity and a landscape that includes 48 lakes within 30 miles and one of the most distinctive industrial heritage sites in the American West. Both towns are genuine, affordable, and off the tourist trail. The choice comes down to landscape: eastern Montana's badlands and river culture, or western Montana's mountains, lakes, and copper heritage.
