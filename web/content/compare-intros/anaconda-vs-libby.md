# Libby vs Anaconda, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Libby and Anaconda offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Libby is a remote timber town in the Kootenai National Forest with 2,775 people, its deep roots in the logging industry, its stunning, rugged isolation, and quick access to the Kootenai River and Cabinet Mountains. Anaconda, with just 9,491 residents, is a historic smelting town known for its towering smokestack, its working-class heritage, and Georgetown Lake and the Anaconda-Pintler Wilderness.

## Who Should Move to Libby?

* Outdoor enthusiasts seeking uncrowded access to some of Montana's most rugged and remote wilderness.
* Retirees and remote workers looking for affordable mountain living far from major tourist hubs.
* People who appreciate a tight-knit, resilient community with a strong working-class heritage.
* Hunters, anglers, and hikers who want to live in the heart of the Kootenai National Forest.

## Who Should Move to Anaconda?

* Retirees and remote workers seeking very low housing costs.
* History buffs fascinated by Montana's copper era.
* Outdoor enthusiasts who want uncrowded access to the Pintlers.
* People looking for a quiet, slow-paced mountain community.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Libby is around $285,000, while in Anaconda it is closer to $210,000, a difference of roughly $75,000, making Anaconda significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Libby averages about $850 per month, compared to roughly $750 in Anaconda.

Libby's overall cost of living differs from Anaconda's. Libby offers lower average salaries compared to Anaconda, with a median household income of $45,000 compared to Anaconda's $48,000.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Libby is a resilient, isolated town tucked into the far northwest corner of the state. It is defined by its timber heritage and its incredible, uncrowded access to the Kootenai River and Cabinet Mountains, offering a quiet, rugged lifestyle. Anaconda is a quiet, resilient town with a deep industrial history, offering incredibly affordable access to some of Montana's best mountain recreation.
