# Red Lodge vs Billings, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Red Lodge and Billings offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Red Lodge is a historic mountain town and gateway to Yellowstone with 2,336 people, its vibrant, historic downtown, its position at the base of the Beartooth Highway, and quick access to Red Lodge Mountain and the Beartooth Mountains. Billings, with just 117,445 residents, is a Montana's largest city known for its major medical centers, its sprawling retail sector, and the Rimrocks and Yellowstone River.

## Who Should Move to Red Lodge?

* Outdoor enthusiasts who want immediate access to skiing, hiking, and the Beartooth Wilderness.
* People seeking a lively, historic mountain town with a strong sense of community.
* Retirees and remote workers drawn to the spectacular scenery and small-town charm.
* Those who want to live in a gateway community to Yellowstone National Park.

## Who Should Move to Billings?

* Professionals seeking corporate or medical careers.
* Families wanting large school districts and diverse activities.
* People who prefer urban conveniences over small-town quiet.
* Those who want easy access to a major regional airport.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Red Lodge is around $550,000, while in Billings it is closer to $375,000, a difference of roughly $175,000, making Billings significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Red Lodge averages about $1,200 per month, compared to roughly $1,200 in Billings.

Red Lodge's overall cost of living differs from Billings's. Red Lodge offers lower average salaries compared to Billings, with a median household income of $58,000 compared to Billings's $65,000.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Red Lodge is a vibrant, historic mountain town that balances its role as a tourist destination with a strong, year-round local community. It offers spectacular access to the Beartooth Mountains and a lively downtown filled with shops, restaurants, and local events. Billings is a bustling regional hub with a true city feel, offering extensive amenities and a fast-paced environment.
