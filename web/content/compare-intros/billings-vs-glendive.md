# Glendive vs Billings: Which Montana Community is Right for You?

Glendive and Billings are both eastern Montana communities on the Yellowstone River corridor, but the scale of difference between them is dramatic. Glendive is the Paddlefish Capital of the World, a community of 4,873 in Dawson County at 2,083 feet elevation, with 30 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 7 viewpoints, 6 museums, 4 fishing access sites, and Makoshika State Park, Montana's largest state park, right at its doorstep. Billings is the Magic City, Montana's largest city with 117,116 residents in Yellowstone County at 3,123 feet, with 53 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 16 fishing access sites, 9 museums, 9 golf courses, 5 historic sites, and the Rimrocks defining its skyline.

## Who Should Move to Glendive?

*   Budget-conscious buyers who want eastern Montana at a very low price, with a typical home value of $185,447 and median rent of just $832 per month, some of the most affordable figures in the state
*   Outdoor enthusiasts and anglers who want Makoshika State Park, the Yellowstone River, 4 fishing access sites, and some of the best paddlefish snagging in the country right in their backyard
*   People who want a small, quiet eastern Montana community with a genuine small-town character, a 1,000-student school district, and a pace of life that Billings cannot offer
*   History and fossil enthusiasts drawn to Makoshika State Park's badlands, dinosaur fossils, and one of the most striking geological landscapes in Montana

## Who Should Move to Billings?

*   Professionals, families, and anyone who needs a full-service Montana city, with a 16,000-student school district, Billings Logan International Airport 5 miles away, and the full range of employment, healthcare, and retail that comes with Montana's largest city
*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 16 fishing access sites, 9 golf courses, 4 state parks, 5 historic sites, and the Rimrocks trail system all within 30 miles
*   People who want a city with real momentum, a growing arts scene, a walkable downtown, and a community that has been reinvesting in itself for years
*   Anyone who wants to be within a few hours of both Yellowstone National Park and the Beartooth Highway

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Glendive is dramatically more affordable than Billings. The typical home value in Glendive is around $185,447, compared to $390,654 in Billings, more than double. Rent follows the same pattern: Glendive averages about $832 per month, while Billings comes in at roughly $1,383.

Median household incomes are nearly identical: $71,063 in Glendive versus $71,855 in Billings. That means Glendive residents get nearly the same purchasing power at a fraction of the housing cost, making it one of the better value propositions in eastern Montana.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Glendive is a small eastern Montana town where the Yellowstone River, Makoshika State Park, and the paddlefish season define the calendar. Billings is a real city, with traffic, a hospital district, a performing arts center, and a community that functions as the commercial hub for a region stretching into Wyoming and the Dakotas. If you want the quiet of eastern Montana at a very low cost of living, Glendive delivers. If you want Montana's biggest city with the most services, the most jobs, and the most amenities, Billings is the clear answer.
