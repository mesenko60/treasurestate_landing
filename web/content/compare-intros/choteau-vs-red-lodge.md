# Choteau vs Red Lodge, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Choteau and Red Lodge offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Choteau is a scenic gateway town to the Rocky Mountain Front with 1,721 people, its spectacular views of the Front, its rich dinosaur paleontology history, and quick access to the Bob Marshall Wilderness and Freezout Lake. Red Lodge, with 2,336 residents, is a historic mountain town and gateway to Yellowstone known for its vibrant, historic downtown, its position at the base of the Beartooth Highway, and Red Lodge Mountain and the Beartooth Mountains.

## Who Should Move to Choteau?

* Outdoor enthusiasts who want uncrowded access to the Bob Marshall Wilderness.
* Birders and wildlife watchers drawn to the spectacular migrations at nearby Freezout Lake.
* People seeking a quiet, affordable community with some of the best mountain views in the state.
* Those who appreciate a traditional, slow-paced rural lifestyle.

## Who Should Move to Red Lodge?

* Outdoor enthusiasts who want immediate access to skiing, hiking, and the Beartooth Wilderness.
* People seeking a lively, historic mountain town with a strong sense of community.
* Retirees and remote workers drawn to the spectacular scenery and small-town charm.
* Those who want to live in a gateway community to Yellowstone National Park.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Choteau is around $288,323, while in Red Lodge it is closer to $550,000, a difference of roughly $261,677, making Choteau significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Choteau averages about $713 per month, compared to roughly $1,200 in Red Lodge.

Choteau's overall cost of living differs from Red Lodge's. Choteau offers lower average salaries compared to Red Lodge, with a median household income of $53,929 compared to Red Lodge's $58,000.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Choteau is a quiet, stunningly located town that serves as the Front Porch of the Rockies. It offers a slow-paced, affordable lifestyle deeply connected to agriculture, paleontology, and the incredible wilderness right in its backyard. Red Lodge is a vibrant, historic mountain town that balances its role as a tourist destination with a strong, year-round local community. It offers spectacular access to the Beartooth Mountains and a lively downtown filled with shops, restaurants, and local events.
