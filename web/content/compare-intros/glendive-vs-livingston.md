# Glendive vs Livingston: Which Montana Community is Right for You?

Glendive and Livingston are both Yellowstone River towns, but they sit in very different parts of Montana and offer very different lifestyles. Glendive is the Paddlefish Capital of the World, a community of 4,873 in Dawson County in eastern Montana at 2,083 feet elevation, with 30 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles and Makoshika State Park at its edge. Livingston is the Windiest City in Montana, a community of 8,040 in Park County in central Montana at 4,501 feet, with 144 outdoor recreation sites within 30 miles, 40 lakes, 24 fishing access sites, 16 waterfalls, 16 campgrounds, 12 trailheads, 7 boat launches, 2 hot springs, a ski area, and Yellowstone National Park 35 miles to the south.

## Who Should Move to Glendive?

*   Budget-conscious buyers who want Montana at a very low price, with a typical home value of $185,447 and median rent of just $832 per month, compared to Livingston's $509,477 and $1,783
*   Outdoor enthusiasts and anglers who want Makoshika State Park, the Yellowstone River, and some of the best paddlefish snagging in the country right in their backyard
*   People who want a small, quiet eastern Montana community with a genuine small-town character and a slower pace of life
*   Remote workers or retirees who want affordable housing and a community where $71,063 in median household income goes a very long way

## Who Should Move to Livingston?

*   Outdoor enthusiasts who want 24 fishing access sites, 40 lakes, 16 waterfalls, 12 trailheads, 2 hot springs, a ski area, and Yellowstone National Park all within reach
*   Artists, writers, and people drawn to Livingston's well-known creative community, its historic downtown, and a character that has attracted writers and artists for decades
*   Families who want a 1,400-student school district and a community with Bozeman's full range of services just 35 miles away
*   Anyone who wants to fish the Yellowstone River, explore Paradise Valley, and have Yellowstone National Park as a regular weekend destination

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Glendive is dramatically more affordable than Livingston. The typical home value in Glendive is around $185,447, compared to $509,477 in Livingston, nearly three times the price. Rent follows the same pattern: Glendive averages about $832 per month, while Livingston comes in at roughly $1,783, more than double.

Median household income is slightly higher in Glendive at $71,063 versus $65,187 in Livingston. Glendive residents earn more on average and pay far less for housing, making it one of the most affordable lifestyles in Montana relative to income.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

Glendive is a small eastern Montana town where the Yellowstone River, Makoshika State Park, and the paddlefish season define the calendar. Livingston is a small central Montana town where the Yellowstone River, Paradise Valley, Yellowstone National Park, and a well-known creative and literary community define daily life. Both towns share the Yellowstone River as a common thread, but Livingston's proximity to Yellowstone and its artistic identity come at a much higher price. If you want affordability and eastern Montana's wide-open character, Glendive is a solid choice. If you want Yellowstone access, mountain scenery, and a creative small-town community, Livingston is one of Montana's most distinctive places to live.
