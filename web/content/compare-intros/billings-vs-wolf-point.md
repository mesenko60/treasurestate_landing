# Wolf Point vs Billings, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Wolf Point and Billings offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Wolf Point is a historic agricultural town on the Fort Peck Indian Reservation with 2,517 people, its position as the largest community on the reservation, its deep roots in farming and ranching, and quick access to the Missouri River and the vast eastern plains. Billings, with just 117,445 residents, is a Montana's largest city known for its major medical centers, its sprawling retail sector, and the Rimrocks and Yellowstone River.

## Who Should Move to Wolf Point?

* People seeking some of the most affordable housing in the state.
* Workers in agriculture, tribal government, or local services.
* Those who appreciate a quiet, isolated lifestyle on the high plains.
* Hunters and anglers who want uncrowded access to the Missouri River and surrounding lands.

## Who Should Move to Billings?

* Professionals seeking corporate or medical careers.
* Families wanting large school districts and diverse activities.
* People who prefer urban conveniences over small-town quiet.
* Those who want easy access to a major regional airport.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Wolf Point is around $145,000, while in Billings it is closer to $375,000, a difference of roughly $230,000, making Wolf Point significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Wolf Point averages about $600 per month, compared to roughly $1,200 in Billings.

Wolf Point's overall cost of living differs from Billings's. Wolf Point offers lower average salaries compared to Billings, with a median household income of $42,000 compared to Billings's $65,000.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Wolf Point is a quiet, resilient community deeply tied to the Fort Peck Indian Reservation and the agricultural economy of northeastern Montana. It offers a slow-paced, highly affordable lifestyle far removed from the state's major urban centers. Billings is a bustling regional hub with a true city feel, offering extensive amenities and a fast-paced environment.
