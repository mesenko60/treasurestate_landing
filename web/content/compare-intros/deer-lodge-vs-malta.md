# Malta vs Deer Lodge, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Malta and Deer Lodge offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Malta is a historic agricultural town on the Hi-Line with 2,100 people, its world-class dinosaur fossils, its deep ranching heritage, and quick access to the Bowdoin National Wildlife Refuge and the Missouri River Breaks. Deer Lodge, with 2,938 residents, is a historic ranching and prison town known for the Old Montana Prison Museum, its deep ranching heritage in the Powell Valley, and the Clark Fork River and the Flint Creek Range.

## Who Should Move to Malta?

* Workers in agriculture, government, or local services looking for a quiet, stable community.
* People seeking very affordable housing and a low overall cost of living.
* Hunters, birders, and outdoor enthusiasts drawn to the vast, uncrowded public lands of northeastern Montana.
* Those who appreciate a traditional, slow-paced rural lifestyle on the high plains.

## Who Should Move to Deer Lodge?

* History buffs who appreciate the town's unique museums and historic architecture.
* People seeking very affordable housing in a quiet, traditional western town.
* Workers in agriculture, government, or local services looking for a slow-paced lifestyle.
* Outdoor enthusiasts who want uncrowded access to the Clark Fork River and surrounding mountains.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Malta is around $244,540, while in Deer Lodge it is closer to $225,000, a difference of roughly $19,540, making Deer Lodge significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Malta averages about $693 per month, compared to roughly $800 in Deer Lodge.

Malta's overall cost of living differs from Deer Lodge's. Malta offers higher average salaries compared to Deer Lodge, with a median household income of $59,449 compared to Deer Lodge's $48,000.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Malta is a quiet, resilient community on the eastern Hi-Line, defined by agriculture, wide-open spaces, and its unique paleontology attractions. It offers a slow-paced, highly affordable lifestyle far from the state's urban centers. Deer Lodge is a quiet, deeply historic town that feels like a step back in time. It is defined by its agricultural roots and its unique history as the site of the original territorial prison, offering a slow-paced, affordable lifestyle in a beautiful valley setting.
