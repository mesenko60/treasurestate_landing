# Red Lodge vs Hamilton, Montana: Which Town Is Right for You?

Red Lodge and Hamilton offer two very different Montana lifestyles. Red Lodge is a historic mountain town and gateway to Yellowstone with 2,336 people, its vibrant, historic downtown, its position at the base of the Beartooth Highway, and quick access to Red Lodge Mountain and the Beartooth Mountains. Hamilton, with just 4,659 residents, is a scenic hub of the Bitterroot Valley known for its historic downtown, its relatively mild climate, and the Bitterroot Mountains and the Bitterroot River.

## Who Should Move to Red Lodge?

* Outdoor enthusiasts who want immediate access to skiing, hiking, and the Beartooth Wilderness.
* People seeking a lively, historic mountain town with a strong sense of community.
* Retirees and remote workers drawn to the spectacular scenery and small-town charm.
* Those who want to live in a gateway community to Yellowstone National Park.

## Who Should Move to Hamilton?

* Retirees and remote workers drawn to the stunning scenery and milder weather of the 'Banana Belt'.
* Fly fishing enthusiasts who want to live near the renowned waters of the Bitterroot River.
* People seeking a vibrant small-town community with a historic main street, local breweries, and community events.
* Outdoor adventurers who want immediate access to the rugged canyons and trails of the Bitterroot Selway Wilderness.

## Cost of Living Breakdown

Housing is where the biggest difference shows up. The median home price in Red Lodge is around $550,000, while in Hamilton it is closer to $516,033, a difference of roughly $33,967, making Hamilton significantly more affordable. Rent follows the same pattern: a typical rental in Red Lodge averages about $1,200 per month, compared to roughly $958 in Hamilton.

Red Lodge's overall cost of living differs from Hamilton's. Red Lodge offers higher average salaries compared to Hamilton, with a median household income of $58,000 compared to Hamilton's $52,917.

## Lifestyle and Vibe

Red Lodge is a vibrant, historic mountain town that balances its role as a tourist destination with a strong, year-round local community. It offers spectacular access to the Beartooth Mountains and a lively downtown filled with shops, restaurants, and local events. Hamilton offers a highly desirable blend of spectacular mountain scenery and a charming, active community. The pace is relaxed but the town is lively, with a downtown that serves as a gathering place for locals and visitors alike, all set against the towering peaks of the Bitterroot Range.
